Game Description:

Horrificus is an dungeon crawler game developed by the Original Gamesters about a corrupt wolf lord who was defeated in battle and must venture his way through many challenging levels. Luckily, chests are scattered around the dungeons that carry valuable weapons to aid you. Power-ups like the speedy croissant are also placed around the castle to help on your short but wicked journey. After preparing, you will find your way to the Princess. Duel her to decide your fate.

Gato Exploso 2023 - 2024

Unjudged OGPC Theme: Cycles Link to Gato Exploso TMS

Game Description:

Gato Exploso is a game about a cat with an arsenal of explosives. He must rescue his parents from the clutches of the mighty, cheesy, Ham Sandwich before they become sandwiches themselves. The player will travel through a procedurally generated world with biomes such as ocean, plains, forest, and hills.

Game Cover:

The Story:

When his parents are kidnapped by the Ham Sandwich, Gato Exploso must talk to a turtle that goes by the name of Squirell to find him. Squirell tells Gato about an old wise turtle in a village that knows the location of the Ham Sandwich. He travels there with the help of Squirell and finds Hammy. Gato and he prepare for a battle to the death. Ready your hand grenades!

Game Enemies and Bosses:

  • Bouncy Triangle
  • Cloud
  • Ostrich
  • Ham Sandwich
The mobs' hostility levels will cycle through, so pay attention to if they are hostile or not. For example, when a Bouncy Traingle is red, it means it is hostile, but if it is green, it is peaceful. Ostriches are mobs found in the open, but another person can join in on another device to play as one. A Cloud is a cloud that shoots lightning bolts at Gato. The final boss is the Ham Sandwhich.

Weapons and Tools

  • Basic Bomb
  • Mighty Bomb
  • Gravity Bomb
  • Landmine
  • Grenade
  • Kazoo
The player will have an unlimited supply of the Basic Bomb; however, all other bombs must be found and will run out. The Kazoo is given to Gato by Squirell and when used, summons Squirell.
