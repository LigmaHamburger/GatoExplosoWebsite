Game Description:

Horrificus is an dungeon crawler game developed by the Original Gamesters about a corrupt wolf lord who was defeated in battle and must venture his way through many challenging levels. Luckily, chests are scattered around the dungeons that carry valuable weapons to aid you. Power-ups like the speedy croissant are also placed around the castle to help on your short but wicked journey. After preparing, you will find your way to the Princess. Duel her to decide your fate.

When two Royal Guard enemies of the same level touch each other, they will merge into one of a higher level. The max level was three. The Princess is the final boss and is surrounded with lots of the Royal Guard.

How Do I Play Horrificus?

Download
  • Downlaod the file from the Horrificus TMS page in the Installation section Horrificus TMS page
  • Extract all the content and open the extracted file in Visual Studio Code
Controls
  • ESDF to move
  • Space bar to swing weapon
  • Walk over the potions to use them
  • Health bar is on the right side of the screen

Gato Exploso 2023 - 2024

Game Description:

Gato Exploso is a game about a cat with an arsenal of explosives. He must rescue his parents from the clutches of the mighty, cheesy, Ham Sandwich before they become sandwiches themselves. The player will travel through a procedurally generated world with biomes such as ocean, plains, forest, and hills.

The mobs' hostility levels will cycle through, so pay attention to if they are hostile or not. For example, when a Bouncy Traingle is red, it means it is hostile, but if it is green, it is peaceful.
